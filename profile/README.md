### About Us

Eng Techno is a Software Solutions Company specializing in _Product Design_ and _Software Development_ such as:

- Mobile Applications
- Websites
- Web Apps & Systems
- All kind of solutions for different industries

We are passionate about creating new ideas and products caring about every single detail in every project we work on. We consider every project we work on as ours, Our slogan is “A single bit of detail can make a difference”.
